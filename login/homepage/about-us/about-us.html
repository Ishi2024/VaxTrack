About Us

Welcome to VAXTRACK: DIGITALIZED INFANT VACCINATION SCHEMING SYSTEM FOR SITIO RONGGOT WITH SMS NOTIFICATION-it is your all-time partner in protecting health and timely immunizations. This innovative digital track of vaccination schedule is meant to take away the hassles of parents and health care providers in scheduling. This is a combined seamless timely scheduling and automatic sending of the expected SMS notifications to make sure no child is missing his or her key vaccines. This way, community health will be taken care of. VAXTRACK believes that health has to be accessible, efficient, reliable for all.



  • - We have regular hours at Barangay lecheria every Wednesday for vaccination; hence the community is constantly immunized. This is done at an interval of one month: to keep immunizing babies and their families. This ensures that health and wellness are protected. It aims to bring timely vaccines in an orderly way that builds a more health-conscious and resilient community. Join us in securing the future, one vaccine at a time.

  • - We are very keen on providing comprehensive health services to our community at our Barangay Health Center. These include blood pressure monitoring (BBP), temperature measurements, weight measurements, correct health record management and immunization. Above all, about well-being, it improves access to health care for people and families as a means to remain healthy and protected.

  • - The City Health Workers are essential to ensuring the health of our community. They are responsible for updating the census for each family or household to capture realistic data on the population and identify those needing help to access health services. Thus, with the information generated, the Barangay can render specific and effective health services to ensure that nobody is deprived of any care they deserve.

  • - Barangay Health Center is headed by Konsehal Gener Casintahan Suelto as his leadership continuously delivers smooth operation and vital services to the community. His partner in administering goes by the name of Midwife Teresita A. Dela Cruz, who is an expert in both theory and practice along with his compassion with this undeniable scenario of addressing the health needs of the residents. They are convinced that through them they will be able to create a healthier and resilient community to the fullest.
